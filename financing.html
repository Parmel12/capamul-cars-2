Financing Requirements

Please prepare the following documents for a smooth and fast approval process.

Principal Applicant (Maker)

  • • Loan Application Form
  • • Three (3) photocopies of two (2) Valid IDs
  • • Two (2) pcs 2x2 Picture
  • • Two (2) photocopies Barangay Clearance
  • • Two (2) photocopies Cedula
  • • Proof of Billing
  • • Proof of Income
  • • Post-Dated Checks (if required)
  • • Marriage Certificate (if married, when required)
  • • TIN Number (some financing companies)
  • • Signature Specimen

Co-Maker

  • • Valid IDs
  • • 2x2 Picture
  • • Barangay Clearance
  • • Cedula
  • • Proof of Billing
  • • Proof of Income
  • • Marriage Certificate (if married)
  • • Signature Specimen
